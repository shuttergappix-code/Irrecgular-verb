How to Play

Controls

Use Arrow Left and Arrow Right, or tap the left, center, or right side of the game.

Mission

Read the mission at the top. Choose the correct irregular verb card.

Score

Correct answer: +10 points. The game increases speed only a little.

Life

Wrong answer: lose 1 heart. You have 3 hearts. The game ends when hearts are gone.
