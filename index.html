How to Play

Controls

Use Arrow Left and Arrow Right on keyboard, or tap the left/right side of the game screen.

Mission

Read the mission at the top. Collect the correct irregular verb form from 3 lanes.

Score

Correct answer: +10 points. The game gets only a little faster.

Life

Wrong answer: lose 1 heart. You have 3 hearts. The game ends when all hearts are gone.

Example

Mission: Collect Past Simple (V2) of “go”

Correct answer: went
